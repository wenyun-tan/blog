Peeling away the curtain on poverty in Singapore

If you’re Singaporean - read this book. If you live in a world where there are people who could own up to twelve cars (cue Selling Sunset), and also people who might not have a roof over their heads - read this book. You will be surprised, challenged, compelled to think, and will keep thinking.
