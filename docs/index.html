Drawing the curtain on poverty in Singapore

If you’re Singaporean - read this book. If you live in a world where there are people who could own up to twelve cars (cue Selling Sunset), and also people who might not have a roof over their heads - read this book. But be prepared: reading TIWILL is uncomfortable. It offers not only a level of scrutiny over the lives of a group of people often considered “invisible”, but also a deep examination into the values and preconceptions of the reader.
